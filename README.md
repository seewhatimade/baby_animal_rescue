# Baby Animal Rescue!

Fly a rescue helicopter, scoop up injured baby animals, heal them at the vet clinic, and reunite them with their mommies — all before the dinosaur smashes the clinic!

https://seewhatimade.github.io/baby_animal_rescue/

## How to Play

1. **Take off** from the helipad at the vet clinic.
2. **Find an injured baby** — there are 8 hidden along the world, with arrow indicators pointing to ones off-screen.
3. **Lower the basket** next to a baby and they'll hop in.
4. **Fly back to the vet clinic** and lower the basket inside the safety zone — the baby is healed.
5. Once all babies are healed, **the mommies arrive** and there's a happy reunion. You win!

## Controls

**Keyboard:**
- Arrow keys to fly the helicopter
- **Space** (or Enter) to lower / raise the basket
- **M** to toggle music

**Mobile:** On-screen D-pad on the left, **CAGE** button on the right to lower the basket.

## Watch Out

- **The dinosaur** stomps in from one side and starts smashing the vet clinic. If it destroys the clinic before you finish, it's game over.
- Babies still in the cage when the clinic falls get **eaten** — get them inside the safety zone!
- The dino can come from either side, so keep an eye on the warning arrows.

## Themes

Each playthrough cycles to a new scene:

1. **Sunny Meadow**
2. **Deep Forest**
3. **Mountain Valley**
4. **Stormy Day** — with lightning flashes
5. **Snowy Day**

Sky, ground, weather, and obstacles all change with the theme.

## Running the Game

It's a single HTML file — just open `index.html` in any modern browser. No build step, no dependencies.
